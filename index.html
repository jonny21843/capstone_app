    About This Project

    This website is designed to make uploading and organizing notes simple and intuitive.

    Users start by navigating to the Upload Files section and selecting a Field, which represents a broad subject area such as IT or Medicine.

    If a suitable field does not already exist, users can create a new one. They then select or create a Category to further organize their notes.

    Once a field and category are selected, users choose a file from their device and upload it. Files are securely stored and automatically associated with the selected field and category.

    Uploaded files can be viewed in the View Files section, where notes are organized based on their field and category, making materials easy to locate and review.
